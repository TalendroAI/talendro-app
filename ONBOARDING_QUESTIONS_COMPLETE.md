## Summary Statistics

- **Total Steps**: 7 main steps (plus alternative comprehensive versions)
- **Total Individual Questions**: Approximately 150+ unique questions
- **Repeatable Collections**: 
  - Employment History (appears in multiple steps)
  - Education History
  - Residential History
- **Required Questions**: Majority of questions are required
- **Optional Questions**: Primarily in Step 7 (Optional Disclosures) and some conditional fields

---

## Notes

- Some steps have both standard and "comprehensive" versions
- Employment History appears in multiple steps with slightly different field sets
- Many fields support pre-filling from resume parsing
- Several fields have "N/A" or "Prefer not to say" options for sensitive information
- Conditional fields exist (e.g., "If yes, please explain" fields that only appear when certain answers are selected)
