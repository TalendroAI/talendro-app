Cliff notes:

Drop the client pack files into your CRA src/ (preserve folder paths).

Add/merge the routes from src/shell/App.js into your router (or replace your shell if you’re using the one from my starter).

On the server, add server/routes.user.js and register it with app.use('/api/user', userRoutes) (example in index.userpack.js).

Run your existing dev scripts. The KPI counts will pull from the provided mock API; the deep links will render stub lists until you wire your data.
