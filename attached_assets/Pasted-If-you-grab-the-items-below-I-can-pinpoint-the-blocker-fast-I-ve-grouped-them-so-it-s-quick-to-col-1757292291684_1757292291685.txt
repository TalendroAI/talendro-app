Likeliest culprits (ties symptoms to causes)

Still seeing “Sample Candidate”: server is in stub mode (AFFINDA_API_KEY not visible to process); or Step-3 is reading a different key than resumeData, or you’re saving the wrong shape (result.data without prefill).

No new logs: stale client bundle (service worker, cached preview) or Replit didn’t restart. The BUILD stamp confirms this.
